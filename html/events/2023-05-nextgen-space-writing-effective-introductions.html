Event details

Writing an effective introduction for your research paper draws your reader in and provides a succinct overview of what will be discussed. Come ready to learn about what content should be included in your introduction and share with others the tips and tricks that work for you!  We will also provide time for everyone to receive peer feedback on an introduction of their choosing from a past class paper or upcoming publication.
