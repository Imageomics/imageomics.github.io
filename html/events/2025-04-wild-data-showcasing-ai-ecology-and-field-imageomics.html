Event details

Join us for a public showcase of student research from the year-long AI & Ecology course! Students will present the results of their projects which use AI methods to investigate ecological questions, built on data collected during a January field course in Hawaii and connected to ongoing conservation efforts on the island. 

Following the presentations, we will enjoy a networking poster session highlighting cutting-edge imageomics research as well as research from students and postdocs from across the HDR ecosystem.

Virtual participation will be available for the student presentations only.
