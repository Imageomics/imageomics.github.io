Imageomics Research Forum: The Cutting Edge of Foundation Models

Event dates

Event details

Imageomics Institute’s Foundation Model project leaders, Yu Su and Harry Chao, will lead us on a journey into the world of artificial intelligence when we delve into the cutting edge of foundation models. They will discuss the latest advancements in model architectures and applications and how our own foundation model will serve as a gateway for advancing biological knowledge through AI.
