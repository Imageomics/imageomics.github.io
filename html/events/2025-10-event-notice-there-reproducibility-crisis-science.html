Event spotlight: Elizabeth Campolongo, PhD—Senior Data Scientist at the Imageomics Institute and the AI and Biodiversity Change Global Center—joins the The Ohio State University Center for Ethics & Human Values CARE Panel on reproducibility in science (Monday, Oct. 20, noon–1:15 p.m. ET, via Zoom).
Elizabeth leads work that makes AI for Nature trustworthy: FAIR, reproducible data pipelines, clear benchmarks, and open workflows others can rerun and build on—connecting ethics to day-to-day research so results inform real conservation decisions.
