Tanya Berger-Wolf and Imageomics at the Aspen Global Change Institute

Event dates

Event details

Imageomics and ABC Global Climate Center Director Tanya Berger-Wolf speaks at the Aspen Global Change Institute and Aspen Center For Environmental Studies conference on July 30.

Her talk, free in person or online, is “AI for Nature: From Science to Impact," introducing a new field of science, #Imageomics, and present a vision and examples of AI as a trustworthy partner both in science and biodiversity conservation, discussing opportunities and challenges.
