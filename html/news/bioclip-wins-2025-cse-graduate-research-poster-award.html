April 3, 2025

BioClip Wins 2025 CSE Graduate Research Poster Award

Congratulations to the “BioCLIP: A Vision Foundation Model for the Tree of Life” team for winning Best Poster at the 2025 Ohio State Computer Science and Engineering Graduate Research Poster Awards.

This recognition celebrates the groundbreaking work led by Ohio State University grad student, Sam Stevens, whose research at the Imageomics Institute focuses on developing AI tools to help scientists understand biodiversity through images. BioCLIP is a vision foundation model trained on the TreeOfLife-10M dataset, enabling researchers to classify and analyze biological images at scale.

Stevens' work advances the core Imageomics mission: unlocking biological knowledge through AI and computer vision to support scientific discovery and conservation.
