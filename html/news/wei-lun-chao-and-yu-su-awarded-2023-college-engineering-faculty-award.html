March 27, 2023

Wei-Lun Chao and Yu Su awarded 2023 College of Engineering Faculty Award

Imageomics researchers Yu Su (left) and Wei-Lun (Harry) Chao (right) were recently awarded the 2023 College of Engineering Faculty Award at The Ohio State University in recognition of their outstanding scholarship. They will be honored along with other College of Engineering faculty at the upcoming awards ceremony of April 27th. Learn more about the awards and past winners.
