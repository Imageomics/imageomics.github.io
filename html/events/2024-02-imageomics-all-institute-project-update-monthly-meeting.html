Event details

We are excited to invite you to the 2024-2025 monthly project updates meeting of the Imageomics Institute. These sessions will take place on the third Monday of each month at 4:00 PM ET and will be an excellent opportunity to hear directly from our students and faculty about the recent progress they’ve made on their Imageomics projects. All Imageomics project members are encouraged to come prepared each month to discuss their current work and progress.

To participate, please register in advance using the link below:

Register for the 2024-25 Meeting Series

PLEASE use the “Add to Calendar” feature in the confirmation email to add the recurring meeting to your calendar. 

You only need to register once for all sessions through June 2025.

We hope you can join us for what promises to be a dynamic and engaging monthly meeting!
