On September 11 and 18, Imageomics will host two connected training sessions covering an important topic that is often left as a part of the “hidden curriculum” for researchers doing computational work. Rather than figuring it out from scratch in your spare time, join us for an interactive introduction to the basics of version control with git and collaboration on GitHub.

Instructor: Dr. Matthew Thompson
