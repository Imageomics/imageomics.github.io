Event details

The Imageomics Institute’s Community and Collaborators Day on April 17, 2025, will bring together researchers and partners to explore the next steps in this rapidly evolving field. Hosted at The Ohio State University in Columbus, OH, this event offers a unique opportunity for meaningful discussions on research, productization, and collaboration.

Expect engaging conversations with fellow collaborators and industry leaders, insights into the latest research and emerging trends, and opportunities to build valuable connections. Together, we’re advancing the field of Imageomics — and your expertise is a vital part of this collective mission.

Looking to Get Even More Involved?

Stay with us for the Imageomics Institute’s Internal All-Hands Meeting on April 18, 2025, and play a key role in shaping the future of this emerging field. This strategy-focused day will dive deep into building a long-term vision for a sustained and thriving field of Imageomics.

Your voice and expertise are essential as we collaborate to set priorities, strengthen partnerships, and chart the next phase of Imageomics research and impact. Let’s build this future together!
