Imageomics team is hiring a program manager

The Imageomics Institute is hiring a full-time program manager to oversee key NSF grant and sub-grant activities that are implemented within the scope of the Institute. Technical oversight, support, and expertise for the grant(s) is provided by Ohio State University (Translational Data Analytics Institute) in Columbus, Ohio and the sub-award partners. The position is located in Columbus, Ohio, but could be negotiated to work remotely. Read the full job posting.
