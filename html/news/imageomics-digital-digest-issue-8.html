Digital Digest Newsletter, Issue 8, 2026

Imageomics Institute's Digital Digest, Issue 8, is out now and filled with our latest milestones to continue pushing the boundaries of AI-enabled biological sciences such as:

  • Presenting cutting-edge work at Neural Information Processing Systems Conference (NeurIPS), including BioCLIP-2, a new foundation model trained on the world's largest biology image dataset.
  • Building community through the upcoming First Imageomics Conference and Tools Workshop showcasing our “pixels to principles” discovery approach.
  • Joining a new National Science Foundation FAIROS award to strengthen AI-ready ecological data infrastructure.
  • Developing new methods that integrate synthetic captions to deepen biological understanding in computer vision.
  • From major publications to expanding global collaborations, Imageomics researchers continue to shape the future of biological research.
