Event details

As part of COSI's Science Festival, we're hosting a family-oriented event on April 29, 2026.

Turn the classic “I Spy” game into a surprising window into how artificial intelligence learns to recognize nature. Through hands-on activities and live demos, explore how computers use visual traits and patterns to sort, group, and identify species from images. Then head outside for a biodiversity scavenger hunt and try AI tools to help identify what you find.

Join us April 29, 4-6pm at the Columbus Metropolitan Library Whetstone Branch. No coding required—just curiosity!
