Event details

In February 2024, we are pleased to announce our inaugural Imageomics workshop as part of the AAAI 2024 conference. Imageomics aims to computationally analyze traits from images, covering morphology, physiology, behavior, and genetic characteristics of organisms. Our primary goal is to ensure that AI models are firmly grounded in established scientific knowledge, enabling them to provide predictions that are both broadly applicable and biologically relevant.

The workshop's broader vision is to foster a community of researchers at the intersection of AI and biology, actively contributing to the development of the field of Imageomics, which is experiencing rapid growth.

We invite experts and scholars from various fields to submit research papers exploring the applications of artificial intelligence and machine learning techniques for deciphering characteristic patterns within image and video data of organisms. Your contributions will play a pivotal role in shaping the future of Imageomics.
