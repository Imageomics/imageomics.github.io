March 3, 2022

Imageomics-related work accepted into CVPR 2022

A new paper on vision-and-language ML led by Imageomics NextGens Luke Song and Jihyung Kil in collaboration with Harry Chao and Yu Su has been accepted into CVPR 2022, the leading conference for computer vision. The paper, "One Step at a Time: Long-Horizon Vision-and-Language Navigation with Milestones," is supported in part by Imageomics and highly related to the multimodal Imageomics foundation model.

CVPR 2022 will be held June 19-24.
