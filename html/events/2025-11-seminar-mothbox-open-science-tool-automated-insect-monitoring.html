Event details

Join us for a seminar hosted by the Imageomics Institute Monday at 4pm ET on Zoom as part of our new Where Are They Now series, spotlighting how members of our extended community have continued to advance their research since first engaging with Imageomics.

Our kickoff session features Dr. Andy Quitmeyer, a participant in the 2024 BeetlePalooza event, who will share his work on Mothbox and how his research has continued to evolve since the event. Dr. Quitmeyer is the Director of Digital Naturalism Laboratories and Affiliate Professor of the University of Washington.
